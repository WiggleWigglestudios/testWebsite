Team Fortress 2

Introduction

Team Fortress 2 is a multiplayer FPS game developed and published by Valve Corporation.

The gist of the game is highly team focus as seen with the title. The game offers nine unique classes to play

as, all with different abilities and playstyles. The game also has a wide range of game modes, with the main

ones being payload, capture the flag, king of the hill, capture the control point, and attack and defend.

Classes

Down below are the nine different classes to play as in Team Fortress 2.

Scout

The Scout is the first of the nine, and uniqueness lies in his agility. For instance, the Scout is the fastest out of all the

nine classes, making him a good choice for flanking and dodging. His primary weapon is usually a type of scattergun, and he is the only

class that can double jump.

Soldier

The soldier’s uniqueness in this game is that he has a rocket launcher as his primary weapon. This allows for a mechanic

known as rocket jumping and market gardening. Rocket jumping is when the player uses the rocket launcher to fly through the air at high-speed rates.

Building on this, market guarding is the art of rocket launching and hitting someone mid-air with an unlockable weapon known as market guarding

(a type of shovel).

Pyro

The pyro is considered one of the most annoying classes to deal with in tf2. The class specialty lies within its wide range of fire-based weapons

ranging from a flamethrower to a flare gun. The class is especially good at dealing with spies. It also has an ability with the flamethrower known

as the “air-blast,” which can deflect projectiles and is capable of deflecting projectiles and pushing enemy characters.

Demoman

The Demoman is a class that specializes in explosives. The primary weapon for the Demoman is the grenade launcher. The Demoman also has

a sticky launch which launches sticky bombs that can be detonated when the user wants. The Demoman also has a secondary playstyle known as

the demoknight. To play as a demoknight, a player must unlock one of the six swords, and a shield that will allow for a charge ability.

Heavy

This guy has a minigun and eats sandwiches.

Engineer

The Engineer is particular because he is the only class that can build buildings to help support the team and is very good at

sending chaos to the other. He can place three different buildings: a sentry gun, dispenser, heals and supplies ammo, and a teleporter.

All of the buildings have three levels.

Medic

The Medic is the main support class of tf2. As the name suggests, the Medic is very good at healing. He achieves this

by using his “medic gun.” There are a total of four medic guns with their different abilities. The abilities include being

invincible, having critical hits, an increased healing rate, and reduced damage depending on the setting.

Sniper

The Sniper can be one of the most dangerous or useless class, depending on the player. By default, the

sniper uses a rifle. However, there is the possibility of unlocking a bow and arrow. This allows for a

different play style of firing arrows instead of bullets.

Spy

The Spy is my personal favorite class to play as in Team Fortress 2. The feature that

captivated me the most, however, is the spy’s ability to one-shot backstab enemies when attacking

from behind with his knife. He can get behind enemy lines via the use of the disguise kit,

which allows him to look identical to whoever he wants, and via the use of an invisibility watch.

The Spy also has a “sapper” which is a device that can disable and slowly drain the health of any

engineer building.

Finishing Touches

In conclusion, this page has barely touched the surface of Team Fortress 2. For instance,

every class has more than a handful of different weapons to use and choose from. I did not

go over the hundreds of hats and cosmetics or the thousands of weapon skins, and I did not go

over the upcoming Halloween event that will start on October 1st. Although if there is one

thing to take away from here, it’s that Team Fortress 2 is a very unique and fun game, and I

would highly recommend trying it. And the best thing is, is that it is 100% completely free.
