CUPHEAD

Cuphead is one of the best games in my (Breck's) opinion. Cuphead falls under many categories such as indie game, shoot em up, platformer, and run and gun. Cuphead has a unique style that may make you think it is a 1930s cartoon. Cupheadâ€™s art consists of 50,000 hand drawn frames! Their music is a nice fast pace swing jazz that comes from the 1930s.

Don't let this cool/unique art style fool you. This game is extremely challenging. The game has 19 different bosses, 6 run and gun levels, and three different islands. I have had the game for 2 years and I still haven't come close to beating the game. While the game can be quickly speed runned for the average person the game is a test of their patients. You will die hundreds and hundreds of times. The game is very frustrating but the satisfaction of finally beating a boss makes up for it.

The game has 2 different playable characters, Cuphead and Mugman. In future there might be one more playable character but as of now these are the only 2 playable characters.

There will also be a Netflix tv show expected to be released later this year.
